About UPSC


  • The UPSC(Union public service commission) conducts a countrywide exam called the Civil Services Examination(CSE) yearly. The purpose of this exam is to recruit the best minds in India for various posts in IAS(Indian administrative service), IFS(Indian foreign service), etc. The exam is a multi-phased one and is conducted in 3 phases. The first phases consists of 2 papers both of which are objective type. the second phase has a subjective pattern with 9 papers in which 2 papers are qualifying and only marks of the rest seven are counted. The final phase consists of a personal interview with the board appointed by the UPSC.
